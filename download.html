We are updating detailed documentation, and preparing download packages and demos of our plant phenotyping system. Currently we have beta testers throughout the world, if you would like to deploy the system please contact us.

Leaf Annotation Tool

Our software tool to annotate plant leaves is available for download as a ZIP archive or from our GitHub repository (updated more frequently).
Detailed documentation and instructions are available in the CVPPP paper and in the user guide
Please cite the Phenotiki LeafAnnotationTool as:

M. Minervini, M. V. Giuffrida, S. A. Tsaftaris, “An interactive tool for semi-automated leaf annotation,” in Proceedings of the Computer Vision Problems in Plant Phenotyping (CVPPP) Workshop, pp. 6.1–6.13. BMVA Press, Sep. 2015.

Sensor

We are preparing download packages and demos of our plant phenotyping system.
Detailed instructions to set-up the Phenotiki sensor are already available in the user guide.
Download Phenotiki-raspistillWeb, the interface to operate the sensor from a Web browser.
Please cite the Phenotiki sensor as:

M. Minervini, H. Scharr, S. A. Tsaftaris, “Image analysis: The new bottleneck in plant phenotyping,” IEEE Signal Processing Magazine, vol. 32, no. 4, pp. 126–131, Jul. 2015.

Annotated data

We are preparing data packages of Arabidopsis images and annotations, suitable for a variety of computer vision applications in plant phenotyping.
Please cite our annotated datasets as:

M. Minervini, A. Fischbach, H. Scharr, S. A. Tsaftaris, “Finely-grained annotated datasets for image-based plant phenotyping,” Pattern Recognition Letters, 2015, Special Issue on Fine-grained Categorization in Ecological Multimedia. In press.
